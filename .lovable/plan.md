
# HaiderTech - Full-Featured Tech Services Website

A modern, professional tech services company website with everything you need to showcase your services, portfolio, and connect with clients.

---

## 🏠 Homepage
- **Hero Section** - Eye-catching headline with company value proposition and call-to-action buttons
- **Services Overview** - Quick preview of main services with icons
- **Featured Projects** - Showcase 3-4 best portfolio pieces
- **Client Testimonials** - Social proof carousel from satisfied clients
- **Stats/Achievements** - Numbers that impress (projects completed, clients served, etc.)
- **Call-to-Action Banner** - Drive visitors to contact you

---

## 💼 Services Page
- **Service Categories** - Detailed breakdown of all tech services offered
- **Service Cards** - Each service with description, benefits, and pricing tiers
- **Process Section** - How you work (Discovery → Design → Develop → Deploy)
- **FAQ Section** - Common questions about your services

---

## 🎨 Portfolio Page
- **Project Gallery** - Filterable grid of completed projects
- **Case Studies** - Detailed project breakdowns with:
  - Challenge, Solution, Results
  - Technologies used
  - Before/After comparisons
  - Client testimonials

---

## 👥 About Page
- **Company Story** - Your journey and mission
- **Team Section** - Team member profiles with photos and roles
- **Company Values** - What drives HaiderTech
- **Tech Stack** - Technologies and tools you work with

---

## 📝 Blog Section
- **Blog Listing** - Articles with featured images, categories, and dates
- **Article Pages** - Full blog posts with rich content
- **Category Filtering** - Browse by topic
- **Search Functionality** - Find specific content

## 📞 Contact Page
- **Contact Form** - Name, email, phone, service interest, message
- **Contact Information** - Email, phone, address, business hours

- **Social Media Links** - Connect on all platforms

## 🎨 Design & Experience
- **Professional & Modern** - Clean corporate design with tech-forward aesthetics
- **Fully Responsive** - Perfect on all devices (mobile, tablet, desktop)
